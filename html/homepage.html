The Football Picking Championship

A unique football picking competition that consists of four different football prediction games, each with its own twist:

Pro Football Pick'em

Pick every NFL game against the spread. Bonus points for big games.

Pro Football Run It Up

Pick one team a week. Score points for building a winning streak and margin of victory.

College Six Pack

Pick six of the best college matchups against the spread with confidence points

College Survivor:

Survivor for college football. Pick six teams to win each week. Each losing reduces your number of picks for next week.

Combine points from all four competitions. Compete to be the Football Picking Champion.
